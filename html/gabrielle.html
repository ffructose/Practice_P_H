Game advice

Gabrielle is a Support with the ability of group buff and debuff. Rush confers a brief Immunity and DEF Up to the entire team while striking all enemies and inflicting ATK Down. Broadside similarly inflicts a mass DEF Down on all enemies.

Originating from Njord, the Norse god of the sea, Gabrielle is a particularly interesting Support Esper and one of the best you’ll get. She’s able to protect her allies from debuffs and debuff opponents in several ways, but she can also deal a fair amount of damage using her SPD stat.

Not only are Gabrielle’s debuffs incredibly helpful, but she’s also not as weak as many other Support Espers are, in terms of both taking and dealing damage. For example, her third ability, Rush, attacks all enemies 3 times, inflicts the enemy with ATK Down, buffs her team with DEF Up, and also grants her team Immunity once at Acencesion Phase 3. Although you will eventually get Support Espers that are better specified for other functions, such as ones that can heal or provide shielding, she’s still one of the best overall and deserves recognition for what she brings to the table. Indeed, she is easily one of the Best Espers for the Fafnir Ritual Miracle.
